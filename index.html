Frequently Asked Questions

Some of the most common questions we get asked

How do I select the right strategy for my content creation needs?

If you're looking for a hassle-free solution, the Multi-Cluster strategy might be the best option for you. If you have a clear understanding of your desired topics, the Single Cluster strategy provides the flexibility to generate content based on your defined keywords. If you want to boost your local SEO, the Local Cluster strategy is designed to generate multiple clusters of locally-focused content.

Will the Local Cluster strategy improve my local SEO efforts?

Yes, the Local Cluster strategy is designed to improve your local SEO efforts. By providing your business information and choosing your desired topics and sub-topics, our system will generate multiple clusters of locally-focused content, complete with the option to include a contact page URL. This will help increase your visibility in local search results, boosting your local SEO efforts.

Is the internal linking option available in the Single Cluster strategy as well?

Yes, internal linking is available in all of our content strategy solutions, including the Single Cluster strategy. You can choose to include internal links between parent and sub-topics within your content, providing a seamless and organized reading experience for your audience.

What are the options for exporting content generated through SCAI?

SCAI offers a variety of options for exporting your generated content. You can export content as Google Docs, which sorts your clusters into easily downloadable documents stored within folders. Additionally, SCAI provides the option to export in JSON format, and also the ability to export directly to WordPress websites.
